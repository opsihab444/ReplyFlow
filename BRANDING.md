## üé® Logo Design

### SVG Logo Components

The ReplyFlow logo consists of:

1. **Circular Background**
   - Gradient from WhatsApp Green (#25D366) to Teal (#128C7E)
   - Represents connectivity and communication
   - Subtle outer ring for depth

2. **Chat Bubble**
   - White gradient fill
   - Rounded rectangle shape
   - Represents messaging and conversation

3. **Reply Arrow**
   - Green curved arrow pointing left
   - Symbolizes automated reply action
   - Dynamic and action-oriented

4. **Automation Dots**
   - Three dots in a row
   - Represents automation and AI
   - Indicates "typing" or processing

5. **Shine Effect**
   - White circle in top-right
   - Adds polish and premium feel
   - Suggests innovation and quality
